# 🚀 E-Commerce Profitability Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes over **620,000 e-commerce transactions (2021–2024)** to uncover **revenue trends, profit drivers, and hidden inefficiencies** across products, regions, sales channels, and customer segments.

The goal was to move beyond basic reporting and identify **actionable insights that improve profitability and business decision-making**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Despite steady revenue growth, the business lacked visibility into:
- Which products and channels are truly profitable  
- The impact of discount strategies on margins  
- Financial losses caused by product returns  
- Customer segments driving long-term value  

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- **Profit leakage** was driven by blanket discount strategies across all customer segments  
- Several products generated high revenue but **low profit margins**  
- High return rates in specific categories increased operational costs  
- Customer segmentation gaps (e.g. “unknown” segment) limited targeted marketing  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Implement **targeted discounting** instead of broad campaigns  
- Prioritize **high-margin products** in promotions  
- Reduce returns through better product quality and descriptions  
- Improve customer data collection for **personalized strategies**  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features (Power BI)
- Revenue, Profit & Profit Margin KPIs  
- Product-level profitability analysis  
- Channel & region performance comparison  
- Discount impact analysis  
- Return rate & financial impact tracking  
- Customer segment value analysis  
- Monthly & quarterly trend analysis  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** – Data cleaning & transformation  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization & dashboard  
- **Excel** – Initial exploration  
- **GitHub** – Version control & documentation  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning Highlights
- Standardized inconsistent date formats  
- Cleaned currency fields and removed invalid values  
- Handled missing data across multiple columns  
- Fixed incorrect revenue and profit calculations  
- Removed duplicates and resolved formatting inconsistencies  

---

## 📈 Business Impact
This analysis demonstrates how businesses can:
- Improve **profit margins without increasing revenue**  
- Reduce operational inefficiencies  
- Make **data-driven strategic decisions**  
- Better understand customer and product performance
